More About Me
Richard Jones Photography was founded in 2016 with the intention of documenting perfect moments, real emotion, and raw beauty. As a professional Wedding Photographer, I’m constantly watching and waiting for unique opportunities to record the spirit of the day. Whichever type of wedding you desire, Richard Jones Photography is there to capture the magic. Feel free to get in touch if you’d like to learn more about my services or my rates.

I’ve always had a passion for photography. Yet, it wasn’t until I specifically started working as a Wedding Photographer that I understood the real power of capturing a once-in-a-lifetime kind of moment. I seek out those intimate and joyful moments, capturing the essence of every couple’s very special day on camera. To have a collection of photos you will cherish for the rest of your life, and for generations to come, contact me today.
